About AyattAI

What AyattAI is. How it works.

Ayatt, pronounced eye-uht, is derived from the Sanskrit word 'Svāyatt,' meaning autonomous or self-governing. Seven layers form the enterprise nervous system for AI — built with ethical guardrails, governance, security, audit trails, and human-in-the-loop controls.

7 Agentic AI Layers

Seven layers. One connected enterprise.

Each layer adds a distinct capability — together they form a nervous system that connects, observes, decides, and acts across the entire enterprise.

01 · Reasoning & Planning
Intelligence

LLMs interpret language, break down problems, plan sequences of work, and generate recommendations. The brain of the agentic system.

02 · Organizational Memory
Memory

Remembers patterns, prior decisions, enterprise history, and customer context — giving agents continuity instead of one-off responses.

03 · Company-Specific Context
Knowledge

Company policies, contracts, CRM records, and domain context brought into every decision so AI acts with enterprise-specific understanding.

04 · System Visibility
Awareness

Connects AI to enterprise systems and signals — observing workflows, tools, transactions, and real-world operating conditions.

05 · Execution & Intervention
Action

Where AI becomes agentic. Agents recommend, initiate, and complete work — escalating exceptions to the right humans when needed.

06 · Guardrails & Trust
Governance

Approvals, permissions, observability, policy controls, and auditability — ensuring enterprise AI is safe, trusted, compliant, and accountable.

07 · Agents Working Together
Collaboration

The highest value — multiple agents working across roles, teams, and processes to deliver outcomes end-to-end.
